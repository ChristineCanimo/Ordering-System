History

Almalika is an Arabic word which means "Royalty". Almalika originated from Kuwait and was founded by an international event designer named Mr. Rodelio A. Bayan. He is also the owner of DreamWorks located in Kuwait. After many years of working in the catering industry, he decided to bring his business to the Philippines and instead of DreamWorks , he named it Almalika.

Almalika opened in the mid year of 2013 and is being managed by Hachii Javier. As of now, its current location is #47 Pulong Bunga, city of Silang, in the province of Cavite. This company offers all kinds of services that gives satisfaction to the clients.
